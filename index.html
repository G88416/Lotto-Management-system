📏 Draw Delta Analysis
Delta = the gap between consecutive numbers sorted within a single draw. Small average Δ means numbers are tightly clustered; large Δ means they are widely spread. The histogram shows how often each gap size appears across all analysed draws, revealing common spacing patterns in the lottery’s history.
🎯 Gap-Based Ticket Generator
Generates ticket combinations driven by gap and overdue data from your saved draw history. Choose a strategy to control how overdue numbers and historical delta patterns influence the picks.
⏱️ Most Overdue 📏 Delta Pattern ⚖️ Mixed 🌿 Gap-Seeded
⏱️ Most Overdue: Selects numbers that have not appeared in the most recent draws — favouring numbers that are statistically “due” to be drawn.
📈 Number Frequency Analysis
Shows how many times each number has been drawn across the selected window. The heat map colours numbers from cold (rarely drawn) to hot (frequently drawn). Ranks and a frequency histogram reveal the most and least active numbers at a glance.
🔢 Digit-Root & Digit-Product Pattern Identifier
Analyses each drawn number using two complementary algorithms:
1. Digit Root — sum of digits (e.g. 36 → 3+6=9, 11 → 1+1=2).
2. Digit Product — product of digits (e.g. 23 → 2×3=6, 42 → 4×2=8).
Both algorithms are integrated to identify recurring patterns and produce advanced, more accurate predictions of the next draw.
